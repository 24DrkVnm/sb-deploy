Another important change or upgradation in technology is the arrival of Network Slicing. It will revolutionize the network system of tomorrow in a great and good pace. Network Slicing is a method to define a virtual or logical form of network architecture which is a widely used technique that comes under the family of software defined networking (SDN) and network functions virtualization (NFV). It utilizes cloud computing and edge computing in its methodology. This method enables service providers to create a strong network connectivity depending upon the need by creating different slices of its own architecture, management and security for serving the purpose. Using it for different smart services leads to the call for varied communication networks and computing technologies at the infrastructure layer. Capability of meeting a mix of diverse requirements at the same time makes network slicing different from existing network techniques. SDN offers effective management of the network functions. Quality of services that have been existing for years is entirely different from network slicing and later one is more advantageous than the former. Thus this technology can bring new customer experience depending upon the end user. Also Network slicing has been identified as the backbone of emerging 5G technology which opens a field for research.
